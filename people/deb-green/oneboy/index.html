One Boy by Deborah Green

Published by Every Day Press

After her husband died when their son was two, Deborah Green sometimes wrote down on random scraps of paper their boy’s words. These notes are the basis of the book, in which one can hear a young child’s voice after incomprehensible loss - and see his growth and affirmation.
